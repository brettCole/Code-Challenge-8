Helping bring comfort and answers to families

Finally a user-friendly apple watch and iphone app that collects helpful data for those with epilepsy and other seizure disorders. Alert family and emergency contacts automatically with included vitals and location. Help is just a tap away. Allow us to help you take your freedom back.

Features of a Reassuring Product

Continuously monitors heart rate for abnormalities. If there is any flatlining before, during or after a seizure, specified family and medical services will be notified.
Time and location of occurrence will be saved. Including constant tracking of person will be displayed for those notified of problem.
All crucial medical information is available to medical services at a tap of the iPhone screen when they arrive. Including, but not limited to, blood type, number of seizures, brain wave activity.
Collects all crucial medical information during seizure. Heart rate, count of seizures, brain wave activity before, during and after. Then sends this information to a remote database to be accessed later by personal doctors and hospital if need be. Obviously you can also access this information at any time.
Your information is of the utmost importance. You can rest assure that only people you want can access it. We use a very sophisticated algorithm that only we use to encrypt and decrypt your information.
